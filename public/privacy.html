Privacy Policy

Vird — Islamic App
Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

Vird is an Islamic app that provides Quran, Hadith, Duas, Prayer Times, Tasbeeh, 99 Names of Allah, Islamic Quiz, and Habit Tracker features. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not require registration or sign-in. No personal information is stored on our servers.

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (language, theme, prayer method, habit progress, memorized names) locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Location Data

The app requests your location permission solely to calculate accurate prayer times and display your local area name. Location data is sent to:

  • Aladhan API (aladhan.com) — to calculate prayer times
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (nominatim.openstreetmap.org) — to display your neighbourhood name

Your location is not stored on any server we control. It is cached locally on your device to reduce API calls.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party APIs to provide its features:

  • AlQuran Cloud API — Quran text and translations
  • Islamic Network CDN — Quran audio recitations
  • Aladhan API — Prayer times and Islamic date
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — Reverse geocoding

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share any personal data with them beyond the location coordinates needed for prayer time calculation.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send browser notifications for prayer and dhikr reminders. You can enable or disable this at any time. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
